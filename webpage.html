About Me

Hi! My name is Anna Morris and I am 22 years old. I was born in Russia and moved to Renton, Washington after I was adopted at the age of four and a half. I have two younger siblings who are biologically related to me and who are also a part of my adopted family. My passions include dance, music, travel, and eating great foods. I am a soon-to-be graduate at the University of Washington where I studied Political Science. Funnily enough, I am actually pursuing a career in advertising, which is not necessarily related to my degree. When I ask other people to describe me as a person, they often say that I am driven, creative, artistic, funny, passionate, and always ready for adventures and challenges.
